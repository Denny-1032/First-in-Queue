# First in Queue (FiQ) — AI Knowledge Base

## About First in Queue

### What is First in Queue (FiQ)?

First in Queue (FiQ) is an AI-powered customer engagement platform built for businesses in Zambia and beyond. FiQ automates WhatsApp conversations and voice calls using AI, so businesses can provide 24/7 customer support without hiring extra staff.

FiQ connects to the official WhatsApp Business API, enabling automated messaging at scale while keeping conversations personal and natural. It combines intelligent AI chatbots with human agent handoff so customers always get help.

Website: https://firstinqueue.com
Support email: support@codarti.com

## Products and Services

### AI Chat Assistant (WhatsApp Bot)

The AI Chat Assistant is FiQ's core product. It is an intelligent WhatsApp chatbot that handles customer messages automatically.

What the AI Chat Assistant does:
- Answers customer questions using your business knowledge base
- Responds in the customer's language (supports 40+ languages)
- Remembers conversation context so responses feel natural
- Guides customers through booking, ordering, or enquiry flows
- Escalates complex issues to human agents automatically

- Sends images, documents, buttons, and interactive messages

What the Voice AI Agent does:

- Transfers calls to human agents when needed

Voice AI is available on the Business plan and above.

Option 1 — Managed Setup (Recommended):

Option 2 — Self Setup:

### WhatsApp phone number requirements

Accepted numbers:
- Mobile numbers not currently registered on WhatsApp
- Landline numbers with voice/SMS capability
- Toll-free numbers

Not accepted:
- Numbers already on regular WhatsApp
- Numbers already on WhatsApp Business App
- Short codes

If your number is currently on WhatsApp:
1. Open WhatsApp on your phone
2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete My Account
3. Wait 24 hours
4. Then register the number with FiQ

### WhatsApp Business API vs WhatsApp Business App

The WhatsApp Business API (used by FiQ) is different from the free WhatsApp Business App:
- API allows full AI automation; the app only supports basic auto-replies
- API handles unlimited messages at scale; the app is rate-limited
- API allows multiple team members; the app works on one device only
- API supports rich interactive messages, buttons, and lists
- API requires Meta Business account; the app just needs a phone number
- API is the professional solution for businesses serious about customer support

### Business profile and verification

Your business name in WhatsApp comes from your Meta Business Manager settings.

Without Meta verification:
- Your business name displays to customers
- All features work normally
- No green checkmark badge

With Meta verification (green checkmark):
- Verified badge shows next to your name
- Higher customer trust
- Not required — this is optional
- Apply through business.facebook.com

## AI Configuration and Customisation

### How to customise the AI assistant

Go to Dashboard → Chat Agent (AI Config) to customise your AI assistant.

Settings you can change:
- Assistant name: What the AI introduces itself as
- Personality: Professional, Friendly, Casual, or Formal
- Emoji usage: None, Minimal, Moderate, or Heavy
- Response style: Concise, Balanced, or Detailed
- Welcome message: The first message new customers receive
- Fallback message: What to say when AI encounters an error
- Operating hours: When the AI should respond
- Escalation rules: When to hand off to a human agent

### Knowledge Base — How to teach the AI about your business

The Knowledge Base is how you teach the AI about your specific business. The AI uses this information to answer customer questions accurately.

What to include in your knowledge base:
- Your products and services with descriptions and prices
- Business hours and location/address
- Frequently asked questions and their answers
- Return, refund, and exchange policies
- Shipping or delivery information
- Contact details and alternative ways to reach you
- Special offers, promotions, or seasonal information
- Any unique policies or procedures customers ask about

Best practices:
- Write in simple, clear language
- Be specific — include actual prices, hours, and addresses
- Update the knowledge base whenever your business information changes
- Test the AI after adding new entries by asking questions as a customer would
- Add information for the most common questions first

### How the AI escalates to human agents

The AI automatically hands off to a human agent when:
- The customer explicitly asks to speak to a human or agent
- The AI detects high negative sentiment or frustration
- The customer's question is too complex for the knowledge base
- A sales opportunity is detected
- The conversation reaches the escalation triggers you defined

During handoff:
1. The AI tells the customer a human will assist them shortly
2. The human agent receives a notification on the dashboard
3. The agent sees the full conversation history and AI summary
4. The agent responds directly via the dashboard
5. The agent can return the conversation to AI when finished

### Operating hours configuration

Set your business operating hours in Dashboard → Chat Agent → Operating Hours.

During business hours:
- AI responds normally and can escalate to human agents
- Voice calls are allowed

Outside business hours:
- AI still responds to messages (24/7 availability)
- AI informs customers that a human agent will follow up during business hours
- No voice calls are sent outside operating hours
- Emergency escalation is still possible if configured

## Conversations and Agent Dashboard

### How conversations work

When a customer sends a WhatsApp message to your business number:
1. FiQ receives the message via the WhatsApp Business API
2. The AI reads the message and checks your knowledge base
3. The AI generates a helpful, natural-language response
4. The response is sent back to the customer on WhatsApp
5. The conversation appears on your dashboard for monitoring
6. If the AI cannot help, it escalates to a human agent

### Conversation statuses explained

- Active: AI is currently handling the conversation
- Waiting: Customer is waiting for a human agent
- Agent (Handoff): A human agent has taken over from the AI
- Resolved: The conversation has been completed and closed

### How to take over a conversation (human handoff)

1. Go to Dashboard → Conversations
2. Select the conversation you want to take over
3. Click the "Take Over" button
4. The AI stops responding and you can type messages directly
5. Your messages are sent to the customer via WhatsApp
6. When finished, click "Return to AI" to let the AI handle again
7. Or click "Resolve" to close the conversation

### Tips for human agents

- Read the full conversation history before responding
- The customer may not know they were talking to AI
- Acknowledge what the AI already discussed
- Take ownership of the customer's issue
- Use the quick reply templates for common responses
- Return the conversation to AI when the issue is resolved
- Add notes or tags to help categorise conversations

## Troubleshooting and Support

### AI not responding to messages

If the AI is not responding to customer messages:
1. Check that the AI is enabled: Dashboard → Chat Agent → make sure "Bot Active" is shown
2. Check the knowledge base: Dashboard → Chat Agent → Knowledge Base should have entries
3. Check WhatsApp connection: Dashboard → Integrations → WhatsApp should show "Connected"
4. Check if the conversation is in handoff mode (human agent mode) — the AI doesn't respond during handoff
5. Check your plan usage — if you've exceeded your message limit, the AI will inform customers
6. Contact support at support@codarti.com if the issue persists

### WhatsApp messages not being delivered

If messages are not reaching customers:
1. Verify your WhatsApp access token is valid and not expired
2. Check that your phone number is properly registered with Meta
3. Ensure the customer's number is a valid WhatsApp number
4. Check if you're within the 24-hour messaging window
5. Review error logs in your dashboard for specific error codes

### Voice calls not working

If voice calls are failing:
1. Verify your phone number supports voice calls
2. Check that call hours are configured correctly
3. Ensure you have sufficient voice minutes remaining on your plan
4. Check the customer's phone number is valid
5. Review call logs in Dashboard → Voice Agent

### Account and login issues

- Forgot password: Use the "Reset Password" link on the login page
- Account locked: Contact support at support@codarti.com
- Cannot access dashboard: Clear browser cache or try a different browser
- Two-factor authentication issues: Contact support for recovery

### How to contact FiQ support

- Email: support@codarti.com
- In-app live chat: Available on Business and Enterprise plans (Dashboard → Help)
- Phone support: Available on Enterprise plan only
- Response times: Basic (24–48 hours), Business (4–8 hours), Enterprise (1–2 hours)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does it take to set up FiQ?

With managed setup (FiQ team does it for you): 1–2 business days.
With self setup (you follow the wizard): 1–2 hours.

### Can I use my existing WhatsApp number?

Yes, but only if it is not currently registered on WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business App. You must delete the existing WhatsApp account from that number, wait 24 hours, then register it with FiQ.

### Do customers know they are talking to an AI?

The AI introduces itself as your business assistant (e.g., "Hi, I'm Sarah from [Your Business]"). Customers can request to speak to a human agent at any time.

### Can I customise what the AI says?

Yes. You control the AI's personality, tone, knowledge base, welcome message, fallback message, escalation rules, and conversation flows. The AI uses your business information to generate responses.

### What happens when the AI cannot answer a question?

The AI automatically escalates to a human agent. It informs the customer that a team member will assist them, sends the agent the full conversation history, and notifies the agent on the dashboard.

### How many languages does FiQ support?

FiQ supports 40+ languages. The AI automatically detects the customer's language and responds in the same language. This includes English, French, Portuguese, Bemba, Nyanja, Tonga, and many more.

### Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. Go to Dashboard → Settings → Plan and click Cancel. Your account remains active until the end of your current billing period. Data is retained for 30 days after cancellation.

### Is there a contract or long-term commitment?

No. FiQ is month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time.

### Can FiQ work for multiple business locations?

Yes. The Business plan supports 2 WhatsApp numbers (one per location). The Enterprise plan supports unlimited numbers. Each number can have its own AI configuration, flows, and knowledge base.

### What if I need help setting up?

FiQ offers managed setup where our team handles everything for you. This is included in all plans at no extra cost. Just sign up and we will guide you through the process.

### How does FiQ compare to regular WhatsApp Business App?

FiQ uses the WhatsApp Business API which offers: full AI automation (vs. basic auto-replies), unlimited concurrent conversations (vs. one at a time), team access (vs. single device), rich interactive messages, and comprehensive analytics. The regular WhatsApp Business App is limited to manual responses by one person.

### Can FiQ integrate with my existing software?

Yes. The Business plan includes Zapier, Make, Google Calendar, and webhook integrations. The Enterprise plan includes custom integrations with any system. See the Integrations section for details.
