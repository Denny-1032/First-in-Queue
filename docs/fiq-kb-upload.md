## AI Configuration and Customisation

### How to customise the AI assistant

Go to Dashboard → Chat Agent (AI Config) to customise your AI assistant. The page uses a tabbed layout:

Tabs:
- Personality: Bot name, personality, tone, emoji usage, response style, welcome/fallback messages
- Knowledge Base: Business information entries the AI uses to answer questions
- FAQs & Instructions: Custom FAQ entries and special instructions for the AI
- Voice & Test: Voice agent settings and "Test your Bot" chat simulator

Settings you can change:
- Assistant name: What the AI introduces itself as
- Personality: Professional, Friendly, Casual, or Formal
- Emoji usage: None, Minimal, Moderate, or Heavy
- Response style: Concise, Balanced, or Detailed
- Welcome message: The first message new customers receive
- Fallback message: What to say when AI encounters an error
- Operating hours: When the AI should respond
- Escalation rules: When to hand off to a human agent

### Knowledge Base — How to teach the AI about your business

The Knowledge Base is how you teach the AI about your specific business. The AI uses this information to answer customer questions accurately.

What to include in your knowledge base:
- Your products and services with descriptions and prices
- Business hours and location/address
- Frequently asked questions and their answers
- Return, refund, and exchange policies
- Shipping or delivery information
- Contact details and alternative ways to reach you
- Special offers, promotions, or seasonal information
- Any unique policies or procedures customers ask about

Best practices:
- Write in simple, clear language
- Be specific — include actual prices, hours, and addresses
- Update the knowledge base whenever your business information changes
- Test the AI after adding new entries by asking questions as a customer would
- Add information for the most common questions first

### How the AI escalates to human agents

The AI automatically hands off to a human agent when:
- The customer explicitly asks to speak to a human or agent
- The AI detects high negative sentiment or frustration
- The customer's question is too complex for the knowledge base
- A sales opportunity is detected
- The conversation reaches the escalation triggers you defined

During handoff:
1. The AI tells the customer a human will assist them shortly
2. The human agent receives a notification on the dashboard
3. The agent sees the full conversation history and AI summary
4. The agent responds directly via the dashboard
5. The agent can return the conversation to AI when finished

### Operating hours configuration

Set your business operating hours in Dashboard → Chat Agent → Operating Hours.

During business hours:
- AI responds normally and can escalate to human agents
- Voice calls are allowed

Outside business hours:
- AI still responds to messages (24/7 availability)
- AI informs customers that a human agent will follow up during business hours
- No voice calls are sent outside operating hours
- Emergency escalation is still possible if configured

## Conversations and Agent Dashboard

### How conversations work

When a customer sends a WhatsApp message to your business number:
1. FiQ receives the message via the WhatsApp Business API
2. The AI reads the message and checks your knowledge base
3. The AI generates a helpful, natural-language response
4. The response is sent back to the customer on WhatsApp
5. The conversation appears on your dashboard for monitoring
6. If the AI cannot help, it escalates to a human agent

### Conversation statuses explained

- Active: AI is currently handling the conversation
- Waiting: Customer is waiting for a human agent (AI stops responding)
- Handoff: A human agent has taken over from the AI
- Resolved: The conversation has been completed and closed
- Archived: Conversation has been permanently archived (hidden from main view)

### How to take over a conversation (human handoff)

1. Go to Dashboard → Conversations
2. Select the conversation you want to take over
3. Click the "Take Over" button
4. The AI stops responding and you can type messages directly
5. Your messages are sent to the customer via WhatsApp
6. When finished, click "Return to AI" to let the AI handle again
7. Or click "Resolve" to close the conversation

### Tips for human agents

- Read the full conversation history before responding
- The customer may not know they were talking to AI
- Acknowledge what the AI already discussed
- Take ownership of the customer's issue
- Use the quick reply templates for common responses
- Return the conversation to AI when the issue is resolved
- Add notes or tags to help categorise conversations

## Troubleshooting and Support

## Multi-Tenant Workspaces

### How workspaces work

FiQ supports multi-tenant workspaces. Each business is a separate "tenant" with its own WhatsApp number, AI configuration, conversation history, team members, and subscription plan.

A single user account can belong to multiple businesses (tenants), enabling agencies, consultants, and multi-location operators to manage everything from one login.

How it works:
- Signup creates a new tenant (business), user account, and free tier subscription
- If you belong to multiple tenants, a workspace picker is shown at login
- The sidebar shows your current business name — click it to switch workspaces or create a new business
- All data (conversations, agents, flows, settings) is scoped to the active workspace

### Switching workspaces

1. Click your business name in the sidebar
2. A dropdown shows all businesses you belong to
3. Click a different business to switch instantly
4. Or click "Create New Business" to set up a new workspace

### Creating a new business

1. Click workspace name in sidebar → "Create New Business"
2. Enter a business name (minimum 2 characters)
3. FiQ creates: tenant, agent record, free tier subscription
4. Session automatically switches to the new workspace

### Team roles

Each workspace has its own team with roles:
- Owner: Full access including billing and workspace deletion
- Admin: Full access except billing
- Agent: Can handle conversations, no settings access

Manage team at Dashboard → Team. Invite new members by email — they receive an invite link and gain access to the workspace.

## What FiQ Cannot Do

FiQ is powerful, but it is important to understand its limitations:

- FiQ does not process payments directly — it sends payment reminders and links, but does not act as a payment gateway
- FiQ cannot make inbound PSTN phone calls (receive phone calls from customers on a phone number) — it makes outbound calls and supports browser-based web calls
- The AI cannot access external websites or databases in real-time — it uses only the knowledge base you provide
- FiQ is not a replacement for a full CRM or ERP system — it integrates with them but does not replace them
- The AI may occasionally give imperfect responses, especially for very complex or ambiguous questions — human handoff is available for these cases
- WhatsApp Business API requires a phone number that is NOT on regular WhatsApp — you cannot use the same number for both
- FiQ cannot send bulk marketing messages (spam) — WhatsApp enforces strict anti-spam rules
- Voice calls are AI-to-customer only (outbound) — FiQ does not operate as a traditional call centre for inbound calls (use the Web Call Widget instead for browser-based inbound calls)
- Voice AI currently supports English only — local Zambian languages (Bemba, Nyanja, Tonga, Lozi) for voice calls are planned for future release
