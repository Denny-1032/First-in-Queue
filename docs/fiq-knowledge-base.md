## 1. Product Overview

### What is FiQ?

**First in Queue (FiQ)** is an AI-powered customer engagement platform that helps businesses automate WhatsApp conversations and handle voice calls. It combines intelligent chatbots with human handoff capabilities to provide 24/7 customer support, lead qualification, appointment booking, and more.

FiQ integrates with the WhatsApp Business API to enable automated messaging at scale, while maintaining a personal, conversational experience for customers.

### Key Value Propositions

- 24/7 automated customer support in 40+ languages
- Reduce response time from hours to seconds
- Handle unlimited concurrent conversations
- Human handoff when AI can't resolve issues
- AI voice calling for reminders and follow-ups
- No technical setup required - fully managed service
- Zambian payment integration (Airtel Money, MTN, Zamtel)

## 4. WhatsApp Setup

### WhatsApp Business API vs WhatsApp Business App

| Feature | WhatsApp Business API (FiQ) | WhatsApp Business App |
|---------|----------------------------|----------------------|
| Automation | Full AI automation | Limited automation |
| Scale | Unlimited messages | Rate limited |
| Multi-user | Team access | Single device |
| Integration | Full API access | Limited |
| Templates | Pre-approved templates | Not available |
| Phone requirement | Any phone number | Must be on device |

### Setting Up Your WhatsApp Number

**Requirements:**
- Active phone number (not already on WhatsApp)
- Meta Business account
- Business verification documents (for some features)

**Setup Options:**

1. **Managed Setup (Recommended)**
   - FiQ team handles technical setup
   - You provide business documents
   - We configure everything
   - Timeline: 1-2 business days

2. **Self Setup**
   - Follow guided setup wizard
   - Create Meta Business account
   - Verify phone number
   - Connect to FiQ
   - Timeline: 1-2 hours

### Display Name & Business Profile

Your business name appears in WhatsApp based on your **Meta Business Manager** settings:

**Without Verification:**
- Business name displays to customers
- No green checkmark badge
- Fully functional for all features

**With Verification (Green Checkmark):**
- Requires Meta Business Verification
- Shows verified badge
- Higher trust with customers
- Not required for basic functionality

**To Configure:**
1. Go to business.facebook.com
2. Navigate to WhatsApp Manager
3. Update Business Profile (name, logo, description)
4. Save changes

### Phone Number Requirements

**Accepted:**
- Mobile numbers
- Landline numbers (with voice capability)
- Toll-free numbers

**Not Accepted:**
- Numbers already on regular WhatsApp
- Numbers on WhatsApp Business App
- Short codes

**If number is on WhatsApp:**
1. Delete WhatsApp account from phone
2. Wait 24 hours
3. Register with FiQ

## 11. FAQ

**Q: How long does WhatsApp setup take?**
A: Managed setup: 1-2 business days. Self setup: 1-2 hours.

**Q: Can I use my existing WhatsApp number?**
A: Only if it's not currently on WhatsApp. Must delete existing account and wait 24 hours.

**Q: Do customers know they're talking to AI?**
A: Yes, AI introduces itself as your business assistant. They can request human anytime.

**Q: Can I customize the AI's responses?**
A: Yes, through knowledge base, personality settings, and custom flows.

**Q: What happens when AI can't answer?**
A: AI escalates to human agents with full conversation context.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, end-to-end encrypted, GDPR compliant, secure servers.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes, cancel in Dashboard → Settings → Plan. Active until end of billing period.

**Q: What's the difference between plans?**
A: Basic (K499): 1,000 conversations, 30 voice mins, 1 number. Business (K1,699): 5,000 conversations, 120 voice mins, 2 numbers + advanced features. Enterprise: Unlimited everything + custom AI + SLA.

**Q: Do I need Meta/Facebook Business verification?**
A: Not required for basic functionality. Green verification badge optional.

**Q: Can FiQ integrate with my existing systems?**
A: Enterprise plan includes custom integrations. Business plan has basic API webhooks.

## 16. Edge Cases

### Number Migration

**Scenario:** Moving from another WhatsApp solution to FiQ

**Preparation:**
- Backup conversation history
- Document existing flows
- Notify customers of transition
- Plan for 24-hour downtime

**Migration Steps:**
1. Export contacts from old system
2. Delete number from old system
3. Wait 24 hours
4. Register number in FiQ
5. Import contacts
6. Recreate flows
7. Test thoroughly
8. Go live

### Account Recovery

**Scenario:** Lost access to dashboard, credentials compromised

**Recovery Process:**
- Contact support with business verification
- Identity verification required
- Account locked during recovery
- New credentials issued
- Review access logs
- Enable 2FA after recovery

**Prevention:**
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Use strong passwords
- Regular access reviews
- Team member offboarding

### High-Volume Events

**Scenario:** Unexpected traffic spike (viral post, sale, etc.)

**AI Response Guidelines:**
- Maintain consistent quality
- Prioritize urgent requests
- Use templates for common questions
- Escalate complex issues
- Queue messages if needed

**Handling:**
1. Monitor dashboard for spikes
2. Add temporary agent capacity
3. Update AI for anticipated questions
4. Prepare template responses
5. Communicate with customers about delays
6. Post-event analysis

### Negative Sentiment Handling

**Scenario:** Angry customer, negative sentiment detected

**Escalation Triggers:**
- Customer uses angry language
- Multiple failed AI resolutions
- Explicit complaint about service
- Request for manager

**AI Response Guidelines:**
- Acknowledge frustration
- Apologize appropriately
- Offer immediate human transfer
- Never argue with customer
- Document for review

**Human Agent Training:**
- Review conversation context
- Acknowledge previous AI interaction
- Take ownership of issue
- Follow up after resolution

### Compliance Violations

**Scenario:** Spam complaints, opt-out requests, data deletion requests

**Opt-Out/Unsubscribe:**
- Must honor immediately (legally required)
- Add to suppression list
- Confirm opt-out to customer
- Document for compliance

**GDPR Data Deletion:**
- Verify identity of requester
- Delete/anonymize all personal data within 30 days
- Provide confirmation
- Exception: data required for legal/tax purposes

**Spam Reports:**
- Meta may limit WhatsApp account if spam rate too high
- Keep spam rate below 0.5%
- Only message customers who opted in
- Honor 24-hour rule for proactive messages

**Documentation:**
- Log all opt-outs and deletions
- Maintain suppression lists
- Regular compliance audits
