## Test Conversations to Try with the Bot

After pasting the business description into Quick Setup and saving, test these in the **Test Your Bot** widget or via WhatsApp:

### Basic Product Inquiry
> **You:** "How much is the iPhone 16 Pro Max?"
>
> *Expected: Bot responds with price R28,999 and mentions screen protector + case included*

### Shipping Question
> **You:** "How long will delivery take to Cape Town?"
>
> *Expected: Bot responds with 2-5 business days nationwide, R99, free over R1,500*

### Payment Plans
> **You:** "Can I pay in installments?"
>
> *Expected: Bot mentions PayFlex, 4 interest-free installments*

### Returns
> **You:** "I want to return my laptop"
>
> *Expected: Bot explains 30-day return policy, asks for order number*

### Promotion Awareness
> **You:** "Any deals on laptops?"
>
> *Expected: Bot mentions WINTER SALE 15% off, code TECH15*

### Price Comparison
> **You:** "Takealot has the Galaxy S25 for cheaper"
>
> *Expected: Bot mentions price match policy, asks for link*

### Warranty Claim
> **You:** "My phone screen is cracked, it's only 3 months old"
>
> *Expected: Bot asks for order number and photo, explains warranty process*

### Escalation
> **You:** "I want to speak to a real person"
>
> *Expected: Bot acknowledges and triggers handoff*

>

> **You:** "Good morning"

> *Expected: Personalized greeting matching the configured tone*

## Dashboard Agent Test Flow

1. Have a customer send messages via WhatsApp
2. Open `/dashboard/conversations`
3. Verify messages appear (auto-poll every 5s)
4. Verify media messages display with correct icons
5. Click ⚡ → select "Greeting" canned response → send
6. Verify conversation status changes to "handoff"
7. Click "Return to AI" → verify AI re-enabled
8. Click "Resolve" → verify conversation moves to resolved
